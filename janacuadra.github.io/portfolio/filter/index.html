FILTER

Filter is a table mirror inspired by the growing trend of manipulating pictures with photo apps for smartphones and tablets, where reality takes a step to the background and the more interesting changes are applied to the original image, the richer it gets. Filter allows the user to experience this first hand and in loco through a view of two different filters: one golden, one blue. The mirror can adopt five different positions in the user’s search for the perfect angle, allowing these said angles to get as close as possible to 90 and 180 degrees.

Filter is produced with amber and blue mirrors, and finished in Ruivina marble, directly from the vora region in Portugal.
