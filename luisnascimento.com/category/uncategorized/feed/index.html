16th september, 2015

Café Martinho da Arcada, Lisbon

Taking part into the official program of the Portuguese Design year 2014/15, the book Portugal by Design has been released.
This publication developed by Silvadesigners, both in portuguese & english, includes the projects of 95 portuguese designers. At the same time, an associated app is released for free on the Apple Store and Newstand by Apple , in order to diffuse this content worldwide.
Jorge Silva was in charge to pick the authors and to design this book. He accepted the challenge of creating one of the possible portraits of portuguese design, through an informal approach, with a rhythm putting in context the work, meaning where it is consumed.
The launch of the book took place the 6th of september at the Café Marthinho da Arcada, Lisbon with the secretary of state for Culture, Jorge Barreto Xavier, the curator of the portuguese design year, Guta Moura Guedes, and the author and designer, Jorge Silva. My project “Filter” was the project selected to be featured in the book.

]]> Design and design award 2014 http://luisnascimento.com/2014/12/20/design-and-design-award/ Sat, 20 Dec 2014 15:16:00 +0000 http://parisdesignviews.com/?p=3743 Design and design award is an initiative from Marc Praquin. Everyday the homepage of DesignAndDesign.com is dedicated to two projects, one in graphic design and the other in product or packaging design. More than a virtual application, this is also a book published yearly with all the awarded project.

“I realised that it is much easier for us designers to create things than to make a name for ourselves. So I decided to offer our community a simple yet powerful tool, completely free of charge, to free us from the monopoly of mainstream media and, at long last, let us speak for ourselves. This is how the Design and Design project got started. Already a reference in more than 160 countries, this tool is now being extended to a non-virtual application: every year, all awarded projects are published in The Design and Design Book of the Year.”
Marc Praquin, Designer

I was reward both in 2014 for the best product design with “Blue Ink”, a shoes cabinet develop with David Rodrigues in December, 13th and for the best packaging design with “Natural” a salt and pepper shaker in December, 16th. These projects are in the Design and Design Book volume 7 which was release December, 14th, 2014.

]]> Pencil for Blackcork http://luisnascimento.com/2014/02/02/pencil-for-blackcork/ Sun, 02 Feb 2014 15:17:19 +0000 http://parisdesignviews.com/?p=3745 BlackCork, Maison & Objet at Now ! Design à vivre

Blackcork is a Portuguese contemporary furniture brand. The essence of the brand is based on the transformation of a material: expanded black cork.
Sofalca, a company from Portugal, manufacturer of black cork expanded agglomerate since 1966, for thermic and acoustic insulation in construction and decoration in architecture, is now design editor: intended to sublimate the subject betting heavily on original and innovative design with the brand Blackcork.

The designer and art director Toni Grilo invited the talented new generation of Portuguese designers to develop the first collection of 2014.
My “Pencil” coffee table was part of this collection and presented during Maison & Objet at Now! Design à vivre from 24th-28th January 2014 with BlackCork.

www.blackcork.pt

]]> ExperimentaDesign 09 http://luisnascimento.com/2009/09/14/the-field/ Mon, 14 Sep 2009 17:20:09 +0000 http://themenectar.com/demo/salient/?p=110 Aside to the Experimentadesign Biennal, Timeless was a special event setting up at the Museu do Oriente in Lisbon, Portugal from September,9th to November, 8 2009. More than an exhibition, this event has been created by Frederico Duarte + Pedrita. Timeless focuses on a theme that is of paramount importance in today’s society, as well in our future as inhabitants of planet earth. Timeless dwells on raising the level of production explores the use of less resources. It invokes not only the analysis of the consumption of raw materials in productive processes, but also the reconsideration of the final product in terms of efficiency and performance.

Designers have the comptetence, the ability to rethink the way in which we are, presently, producing artefacts – wether tangible or intagible – intended for our needs, simultaneously proposing new methods, new solutions and new formats, therefore making our daily life easier and lowring the consumption of resources.

Cartão desCrédito is a project made by The Office (Luis Nascimento + David Rodrigues), that includes one website and equipment developed in cardboard aiming to propose a new way to rethink consumer society and design challenges.
